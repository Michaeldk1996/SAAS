How it works

From rating to edge in three steps

1
We rate the field
Elo ratings updated daily from ATP results, weighted by surface and recency across 62 tour players.
2
We price the match
The rating gap becomes a fair win probability and a fair odds line for both players, on the surface being played.
3
You spot the value
Fair line meets the market. Where the bookmaker overpays, the board flags a positive edge — you decide.

Questions, answered

Do you place bets or take my money to bet?
No. BSP Consult is an analytics tool. We show fair prices and where the market disagrees — you place your own bets with your own bookmaker.
Where does the data come from?
ATP match results and Infosys ATP serve/return/under-pressure stats, plus market prices from the major bookmakers. Ratings refresh daily.

Edge gives you the full board, ratings and analysis. Pro adds the value engine — model-vs-market edge, line movement, the odds calculator, reports and alerts.
