Methodology

How every signal will be built — no black box, no guesswork.

Current status: the dashboard is live with real odds data. The scoring model described below isn't wired in yet — this page describes the methodology being built, not something already running.

📊 W/UE ratio

We track the winners-to-unforced-error ratio for every player, broken down by surface rather than as a single career average. A player's clay-court ratio can differ enormously from their grass-court ratio, and treating them as one number hides exactly the edge we're looking for.

🎯 First serve

First-serve percentage on its own means little — what matters is what happens behind it. We combine serve percentage with points won behind the first serve, over a rolling 12-month window, so recent form is weighted more than a stale season-old average.

🏟️ Surface form

Results are scored separately for clay, hard, and grass. A player in great overall form but historically weak on the surface being played is flagged rather than assumed to carry their form across.

⚡ Fatigue & schedule

Cumulative time on court and consecutive rounds played are factored into every score. A player through three long three-setters is treated differently from one who's had routine straight-set wins, even if the head-to-head record favors them on paper.

✓ How we validate

Every signal we publish is logged before the match starts and kept on the Track Record page regardless of outcome. We don't remove or retroactively edit past calls.
